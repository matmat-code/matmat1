Olympus Mons

it is the tallest mountain/volcano in the solar system.
it is 26km in height and 624km in width(everest:8.8km in height).
it may still be active.
it is 115 million years old.

Polar ice cap(north)

the polar ice cap is made of frozen carbon dioxide and water.
it can be as cold as –153° Celsius.
it is 400km in width.

Valles Marineris

it is the largest system of canyons in the Solar system.
it is 7km deep.
it is 4000km long.
it is approximatly 200km in width.
